REPORTING INCIDENT

Bullying, sexual harassment and intimidation includes any intentional conduct, including verbal, physical or written conduct, or an intentional electronic communication, that creates a hostile educational environment by substantially interfering with a student’s educational benefits, opportunities or performance or with a student’s physical or psychological well-being.

If you are a student, the parent/guardian of a student, a close adult relative of a student and wish to report an incident of alleged bullying, sexual harassment or intimidation, fill the form on our YFP website or contact the Students Defender at info@studentsdefender.org
