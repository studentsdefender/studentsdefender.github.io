STUDENT RELIEF FUND

The Students Defender- Student Relief Fund is designed to assist enrolled students with a financial emergency in the current term that is impacting their ability to stay on track towards graduation. Students must be either in danger of withdrawing due to an unexpected, temporary financial hardship resulting from an emergency or crisis situation; or struggling with debilitating financial circumstances which may impede their academic progress and ability to complete classes.

Guidelines for Funding

  • Student must be currently enrolled in any HEC recognized University. Grants are awarded on a one-time basis. Relief Fund will not be used for fees. Maximum Relief Fund is Rs.30,000.
  • The student’s academic record will be reviewed for commitment and progress. Although there is no minimum GPA or number of completed units required, the student’s record may be taken into consideration.
  • Need must be verified by a Youth for Pakistan Campus Management Board, Need must be unexpected, short-term or temporary, Fund will enable student to continue with immediate studies.
