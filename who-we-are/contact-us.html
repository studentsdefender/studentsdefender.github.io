Defend, educate and sustain the fundamental rights of students by transforming the culture that perpetuates harassment and discrimination in all its forms at Pakistan’s colleges and universities. We envision a Ideal environment where all students have the freedom to move through campus and public space, participate in academic life, and reach their full potential.
